# 🚀 Create a New Job Posting
Follow the steps below to post a new job using the AgentR platform:

---

## 🧭 Step 1: Navigate to the Dashboard

- Visit [https://app.agentr.global/admin/dashboard](https://app.agentr.global/admin/dashboard)
- If there are no jobs posted yet, you'll see the message:
  
  > “No job listing found”

![Dashboard Empty](../images/dashboard-empty.png)

---

## ➕ Step 2: Click on **Create Job**

- Click the `+ Create Job` button on the top right.
- This will navigate you to the job creation form.

![Create Job Page](../images/create-job-page.png)

---

## ✍️ Step 3: Fill in the Job Details

- **Job Title**: Enter the position title, e.g., `Front end developer`.
- **Job Description**: Provide a clear and concise description for the role.

![Filling Details](../images/fill-job-details.png)

---

## 📤 Step 4: Publish the Job

- Click `Publish Now` to make the job live.
- Alternatively, you can save it as a draft if you want to come back later.

![Job Published](../images/job-published.png)

---

## ✅ Step 5: Job is Now Live

- A confirmation modal will appear saying:

  > “🎉 Boom! Your job post is live.”

- You’ll be provided with a custom job application link.
- Share the job via:
  - X (formerly Twitter)
  - LinkedIn
  - Or by copying the link directly.

![Live Confirmation](../images/job-live-modal.png)

---

## 🗂️ Step 6: View in Dashboard

- Once posted, the job appears under **Available Jobs**.
- You can see:
  - Title
  - Number of applicants
  - Short description
  - Posting time

![Dashboard with Job](../images/job-listed.png)

---

## 🔗 Step 7: Share Your Job Post

- Click on the **Share Icon** on the job card to quickly share the job on various platforms or copy the application link.

![Share Option](../images/share-icon.png)

---

That’s it! You’ve successfully created and shared a job post using AgentR. 🎯
