### 📝 1. Create Your Account

Start by creating your account [here](https://app.agentr.global/admin/signup). You will need a business email address to be able to proceed.

![Create Account](../../images/create-account.png)

---

### 📧 2. Verify Your Email

After signing up, you'll receive a verification code in your email. Enter it on the next screen to confirm your email address.

![Verify Email](../../images/verify-email.png)

---

### 🏢 3. Set Up Your Organization

Once your email is verified, you'll be asked to set up your organization. This includes the organization name, description, and logo.

![Organization Setup](../../images/organization-setup.png)

---

---

### ✅ 4. You're All Set\!

Your organization setup is complete\! 🎉

You can now start creating job listings, reviewing applicants, and streamlining your hiring process right from the AgentR dashboard.

Need help? Explore more topics in our documentation or reach out to support.
